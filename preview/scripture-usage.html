Current Usage Policy

  • Selected NRSVue quotations are used in limited quantity on flagship overview pages only.
  • Claim pages generally use reference-focused scripture display plus a Bible Gateway link for full context.
  • The project tracks verse usage centrally in notes/verse-budget.md.
  • The preview site does not introduce new large scripture quotations beyond what already appears in the underlying markdown files.
