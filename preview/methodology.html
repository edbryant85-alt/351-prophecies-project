Preview Note

This page summarizes the project’s current evaluative criteria and explains how scripture display is handled in the preview prototype.

Evaluation Criteria

Each prophecy will be evaluated using these six criteria:

  1. Prior — The text must clearly predate the event
  2. Predictive Intent — It must be intended as a forward-looking prediction
  3. Non-Mundane — It must describe something unlikely or not easily generalized
  4. Specific and Verifiable — It must point to a clear, identifiable event
  5. Clarity — It must not be overly symbolic or open to multiple interpretations
  6. Independence — It must not be fulfilled through deliberate or influenced action

Scripture Display Policy

  • Overview pages use selective quotation from the current project content and highlight the focus verses most relevant to messianic claims.
  • Claim pages remain reference-focused and generally avoid large quotations, instead linking readers out to Bible Gateway for the full passage.
  • The project tracks verse usage centrally to maintain a low quotation footprint and consistent attribution practice.
