This page reflects the current project methodology and shows how a final site could surface the evaluation grid alongside individual prophecy entries.

The Six Criteria

  1. Prior — The text must clearly predate the event.
  2. Predictive Intent — It must be intended as a forward-looking prediction.
  3. Non-Mundane — It must describe something unlikely or not easily generalized.
  4. Specific and Verifiable — It must point to a clear, identifiable event.
  5. Clarity — It must not be overly symbolic or open to multiple interpretations.
  6. Independence — It must not be fulfilled through deliberate or influenced action.

Prototype Role

In a finished site, these criteria could sit in the background as the project’s controlling method, while each overview or claim page shows which criteria are most disputed. For now, this preview simply demonstrates how the methodology can live in the same visual system as the passage pages.
