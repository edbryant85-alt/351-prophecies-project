# Methodology

Each prophecy will be evaluated using these six criteria:

1. Prior — The text must clearly predate the event
2. Predictive Intent — It must be intended as a forward-looking prediction
3. Non-Mundane — It must describe something unlikely or not easily generalized
4. Specific and Verifiable — It must point to a clear, identifiable event
5. Clarity — It must not be overly symbolic or open to multiple interpretations
6. Independence — It must not be fulfilled through deliberate or influenced action
